About Lertisento

Lertisento provides recruitment and labour rental for Finnish companies that need reliable workforce. We help you fill gaps fast with vetted specialists, and we stay in touch during the project to support onboarding, communication and on-site requirements.

How do we work?

Request

Send a request — we confirm requirements, timelines, and the specialist’s start date.

Selection

We match you with vetted specialists (welders, assemblers, operators, general workers and more).

Start & support

The specialist starts on site — we support onboarding and follow project requirements.
